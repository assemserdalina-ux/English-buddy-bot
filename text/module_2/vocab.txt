Short explanations:
exercise – physical activity that keeps you healthy and strong
training – regular practice for a sport
warm-up – light activity before harder exercise
stretch – move your muscles to make them longer and looser
injury – damage to your body (for example, after an accident)
bruise – a dark mark on the skin after you hit something
sprain – hurt a joint by twisting it
bandage – cloth you put around an injured part of the body
first aid – basic medical help you give immediately
race – competition of speed
athlete – person who does sports, often professionally
referee – person who controls a game
score – the number of points or goals in a game
pitch / court / pool – places where you play football, tennis, swim
healthy lifestyle – way of living that keeps you fit and well
fit – strong and healthy
exhausted – very, very tired
